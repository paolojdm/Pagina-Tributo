Leroy Jethro Gibbs

The man who saved billions of lives

Here is a timeline of the life of NCIS Special Agent Leroy Jethro Gibbs:

  • 1954 born on November 21 in Stillwater, a small town in Pennsylvania
  • 1972 he joined the Marines and left the country in 1976, becoming one of the best marksmen
  • 1991 - his first wife, Shannon, and daughter, Kelly, were murdered while he was abroad engaged in the Gulf War and their death will profoundly affect Leroy Jethro, pushing him to join NCIS Not long after returning from Iraq he was honorably discharged from the Marine Corps with the rank of gunnery sergeant and joined the Naval Investigative Service, becoming part of Mike Franks' team, with whom he would become good friend
  • 2001 meeting a 25-year-old boy named Anthony Dinozzo, he helps him solve a case in Baltimore, later hiring him into NCIS on a permanent basis
  • 2003 Foiling a terrorist attack on a plane, he meets Kate Todd and, amazed by her intelligence, hires her into NCIS, for him Kate will be like a second daughter
  • 2005 - in a huge challenge to stop the terrorist Ari Haswari, he loses Agent Todd to a blow to the head, he will try to avenge her in all possible ways, until he meets the terrorist's sister Ziva David and makes her understand that her brother was on the side wrong, Ziva kills her brother, saving Gibbs' life
  • 2008 During the investigation into the death of NCIS Special Agent William Decker which was believed to be due to natural causes but was likely homicide, Jenny Sheppard, director of NCIS and both Gibbs' friend and old flame, had planned to meet with Franks in a diner, but the assassins arrived first and Jenny, already suffering from cancer, died, this marked Jethro's life even more, who was not only angry with the criminals, but also with Jenny who had not told him nothing about the tumor, in this period the director of NCIS changed and there was, indefinitely, director Leon Vance
  • 2011 - the team is always the same Gibbs, Dinozzo, McGee, an agent officially hired since 2005, Ziva, over the years the four have foiled terrorist attacks, made trips between Iraq and America, solved murder crimes (one of which it was too much noise), met Mike Franks, helped their medical examiners Doctor Donald Mallard (Ducky) and Doctor James Palmer (Jimmy), solved the murder case of Mike Franks' son, met the FBI agent Fornell, and his daughter Emily, however, a serial killer, believing Gibbs to be his main enemy, killed Mike Franks, another death that marked the life of Gibbs, who however managed to capture the serial killer
  • 2013 - after the death of Eli David, director of the Mossad, his daughter Ziva asks Gibbs for authorization to go to Europe where Bodnar, believed to be her father's killer, is located. In the meantime, a Mossad agent is killed and the new director (a woman of whom Ziva will reveal a long relationship she had in her youth with her father Eli David) is in the United States to collaborate in the search for Ilan Bodnar. Ziva and Tony leave for Europe, but not for Rome where everyone believes Bodnar is, but for Berlin where Ziva thinks Bodnar actually is, because that's where she can change her money into diamonds and then disappear. The investigations conducted in parallel by the Mossad, the CIA, the FBI and the NCIS, seem to be groping in the dark. In Berlin, Ziva and Tony instead of finding Bodnar find his brother who helps him. In the meantime it turns out that Ilan Bodnar never left the United States after the murder of Eli David, as the Mossad already knew, but had not revealed it to NCIS. Tony and Ziva then return to the United States with Bodnar's brother and, as soon as they arrive, they suffer a serious (intentional?) collision from an SUV.
  • 2014 - Explosion at an event in Washington, the Secretary of the Navy is killed. The team is suspended from duty, due to Secret Service agent Parsons, but Gibbs is sent to Iran to investigate the case of the marine's head full of radiation, but once there Gibbs ends up in an ambush and is saved by Parsons. Agent Fornell informs the team that the attacks are the work of a terrorist group whose leader is Parsa. In the meantime, Di Nozzo tries to track down Ziva with whom he exchanges text messages. Not succeeding, he turns to Adam, Ziva's contact he met in Berlin. Ziva's traces lead to a safe house where, however, three bodies are found, but no trace of Ziva, only her necklace, a sign that she had been there. Meanwhile, the team manages to understand that the real attacker is Mendez himself, who Gibbs kills. Ziva and Tony say goodbye despite Tony's insistence that she come home with him.
